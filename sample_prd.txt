Feature: User Login

As a user, I want to login so that I can access the dashboard.

Requirements:
1. Login page has fields: Username, Password.
2. Username is required; min length 3; max length 50.
3. Password is required; min length 8.
4. On valid credentials, redirect to /dashboard and show message "Welcome".
5. On invalid credentials, show error "Invalid username or password" and do not redirect.
6. Account locks after 5 consecutive failed attempts for 15 minutes.
7. Must support mobile view.
8. Security: Do not reveal whether username exists.
